The Nexus Gaming Series

The Nexus Gaming Series is dedicated to providing the professional quality esports tournaments, leagues and content for amateur and semi-professional players in the Blizzard title, 'Heroes of the Storm'.

I have contributed code to the Nexus Gaming Series' enterprise scale web project. A website for tracking and managing all aspects of the competitive side of their business, including the scheduling and managing of all the teams participating and their statistics.

The Nexus Gaming Series Core Web Applications is being constructed using Ruby on Rails and SASS. I primarily worked as a front-end developer on the project making contributions to the mobile responsiveness of the website. I also created the the initial Nexus Gaming Series one-pager website.

M.E. Price Fine Art

M.E. Price Fine Art is a web gallery for displaying the artwork of master painter and encaustic designer M.E. Price.

I am constructing a web gallery that may, in the future, expand into a full shop. It has been a lot of fun to learn new ways to do CRUD operations. I have found a personal love for JavaScript fetch through this project, and learned quite a lot about the inner workings of React.

M.E. Price Fine Art has been entirely built in the MERN stack utilizing React, Node, Express, Mongoose, and more.

The Geeze

This project is for the twitch.tv personality The Geeze. I was tasked with taking everything that encompassed Geeze's online presence and making it all accessible from one place.

The Geeze is an exciting and extravagant personality and I worked to capture that in the website. I also was able to incorporate my skills with Adobe Creative Suite by creating custom videos and images for the site.

The Geeze Website is built in JavaScript, JQuery, and CSS on the front end with an Express/ Node back end. I used the back end primarily for scraping data from YouTube to display in the videos section.
